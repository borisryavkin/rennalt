Connect the Printer to the Evolt 360 Body Scanner

  
    
Having trouble with printing or the scan results are not aligning to the result sheets

   

Ensure that the Evolt is turned on

Ensure the printer is turned on and not on Deep Sleep

Connect the printer to the scanner using the provided cable

The USB A port should be plugged into one of the USB ports behind the Display screen, while the USB B port should be plugged into the printer.

Go to the Control Panel by pressing the Evolt logo on the top left corner of the Log In/ Sign up Screen

Select the 'Printer' button

If you are seeing a list of different printers, select each printer and select the ‘Delete Printer’ option.

Once every printer on the list is deleted, click the ‘Add Printer’ option in blue

From the ‘Add Printer’ screen, locate the section of printers listed ‘local printers’, select the Brother HL-L2370DW series option and hit continue

Next screen hit continue

On the next screen, find the list of printer models and choose the 'Brother HL-L2300D' using brlaser v4 (en)’ then tap on 'Add Printer'.

Tip: It is the one after the ‘Brother HL-8050N Foomatic/Postscript (en)’ option and before the ‘Brother MC 3000 Foomatic/epson (recommended) (en)’  

On the ‘Set Printer Options’ page select ‘Set Default Options’

On the ‘Brother_HL_L2370DW_series’ Page select the green ‘Set As Server Default’ button.

In the top left corner of the screen, hit the blue ‘Back to Evolt Control Panel button

Verify that your printer is connected by checking the Control Panel under the Default Printer section - the name should be Brother_HL-L2370DW_series.
